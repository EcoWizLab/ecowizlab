

# EcoWizLab — Landing Page

A bold, colorful landing page for EcoWizLab, an Ethereum client research and zero-knowledge proofs startup.

## Design Style
- **Bold & colorful**: Vibrant gradient backgrounds (purple → blue → teal), bright accent colors, modern SaaS aesthetic
- Glassmorphism cards, smooth scroll animations, gradient text headings
- Dark base with colorful gradients for contrast

## Sections

1. **Hero** — Large gradient headline ("Pioneering Ethereum Client Research & Zero Knowledge Proofs"), subtitle, two CTA buttons (Get Started / Learn More), abstract geometric/crypto-themed decorative elements

2. **About** — Brief company mission statement, what EcoWizLab does (Ethereum client optimization, ZK proof systems), key stats (e.g., projects shipped, research papers, team size) displayed as animated counters

3. **Research Areas / Services** — Card grid showcasing core focus areas:
   - Ethereum Client Development
   - ZK Proof Systems
   - Protocol Security Audits
   - Consensus Research
   Each card with an icon, title, and short description

4. **Team** — Grid of team member cards with avatar placeholders, names, roles, and social links

5. **Contact / CTA** — Final call-to-action section with a contact form (name, email, message) and links to social channels

## Navigation
- Sticky top navbar with logo, section links, and a "Contact Us" button
- Smooth scroll to sections
